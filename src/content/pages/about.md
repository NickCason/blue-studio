I’m Nina. ✨

I’m a single mother, creator, photographer, writer, cosplay gremlin, and professionally curious person with approximately twelve rotating hobbies at any given time.

Most of my life exists somewhere between creativity and controlled chaos. One minute I’m editing content or building fantasy worlds, and the next I’m reorganizing my craft supplies at 1 AM because apparently that felt urgent to my soul.

I’m deeply inspired by storytelling in all its forms. Fantasy books with worn pages, tabletop games that spiral into emotional damage, Renaissance Faires, late-night creative ideas, cozy spaces, strange little trinkets, and people who care deeply about things. Especially the weird things.

I’m also raising two amazing kids, which means my life is equal parts heartfelt moments, school pickup lines, unfinished coffees, and someone yelling “Mom!” through a closed bathroom door.

Over the years, I’ve fallen in love with creating things that make people feel something. Photography. Writing. Costumes. Tiny magical details. Content that feels human instead of perfect.

I don’t really believe creativity has to fit into one box. Neither do people.

So this little corner of the internet has become a collection of all the things I love:

🖤 fantasy

🎲 D\&D and gaming

📸 photography and content creation

✨ cosplay and crafting

🌿 soft healing and self-reinvention

📚 stories in every form imaginable

At the end of the day, I think most of us are just trying to become ourselves a little more honestly than we were the year before.

That’s what I’m doing here too.

So welcome. I’m really glad you found your way here. 🕯️
